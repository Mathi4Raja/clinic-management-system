# CMS Sanity Test Protocol (Manual)

This document outlines the core business flows that must be verified manually to ensure the "sanity" of the Clinic Management System.

## 🟢 1. Authentication & RBAC Sanity
- [ ] **Login**: Attempt login with `admin@clinic.com` (admin123).
- [ ] **Adaptive UI**: Verify sidebar/metrics change when logging out and in as `doctor1@clinic.com` (password123).
- [ ] **Unauthorized Access**: Manually try reaching `api/admin/audit.php` while logged in as a Patient. It should return a `403 Forbidden`.

## 🔵 2. Patient Flow (Front Desk & Self-Service)
- [ ] **Registration**: Logged in as Receptionist, register a new patient "John Doe".
- [ ] **Discovery**: Logged in as "John Doe", view the list of available doctors.
- [ ] **Booking**: Book an appointment with Dr. Sarah Connor for tomorrow at 10:00 AM.

## 👨‍⚕️ 3. Clinical Workflow (Doctor & Lab)
- [ ] **Queue Management**: Logged in as Dr. Sarah Connor, verify "John Doe" appears in the live queue.
- [ ] **Diagnosis**: Select John Doe, enter "Mild Flu", and save the medical record.
- [ ] **Lab Order**: From the diagnosis form, order a "Full Blood Count".
- [ ] **Lab Completion**: Logged in as Lab Tech, find John Doe's blood count request and upload a sample `report.pdf`.

## 💊 4. Services & Billing (Pharmacy)
- [ ] **Prescription Visibility**: Logged in as Pharmacist, verify John Doe's prescription appears (if generated by Doctor).
- [ ] **Dispense & Bill**: Click "Dispense & Bill".
- [ ] **Audit Trail**: Logged in as Admin, check the "System Audit" to see if total revenue and patient counts updated correctly.
